Abraham Lincoln High School Honor Platoon

This is the Abraham Lincoln High School's Honor Platoon. They have gone to nationals two years in a row to San Antonio, Texas and Louisville, Kentucky. Back in 2016 they went out to San Anonio, Texas to compete for the first time. They ended up qualifing for nationls in Loisville, Kentucky by placing 5th.
